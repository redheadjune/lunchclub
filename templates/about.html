About Lunch Club @ {{ config['org_name'] }}

As {{ config['org_name'] }} continues to grow it becomes harder and harder to know everyone on each team at all, let alone on a personal level. Lunch Club is a way to get to know and keep in touch with your fellow teammates in a structured and rewarding manner.

How does it work?

Every month all active members of Lunch Club will be assigned a clique of 1-4 other members. The goal is to go out with each member of your clique in turn over the course of the month (avg 1 lunch per week). Ideally by the end of each month all pairwise combinations of members in the clique will have gone to lunch together. As a reward for cliques that succeed in this goal {{ config['org_name'] }} will sponsor a lunch (up to $20/member) for the entire clique to go out together.

FAQ

Can I go out with everyone at once?

The goal of the program is to foster personal relationships between you and your coworkers. This is best achieved by having one-on-one time where you can really get to know the other person. You don't have to leave {{ config['org_name'] }}, but it would be best to grab a two person table if you choose to stay in to keep the experience personal.

How do you verify lunches happened?

You are on the honor system. Right now anyone can confirm a lunch with and for anyone else. Please only confirm lunches that you participated in. Remember, we play well with others :)

How do we get the free lunch?

One person in the clique pays for everything and can expense it. Make sure you note the expense as Lunch Club and note the other members of the clique. Make sure you only do this if you have a completed clique!

How did you build this site?

My hackathon project was to learn flask, d3 and some front end. This is the result, and it is a work in progress. If you would like to see the code check out github.com/sc932/lunchclub. Pull requests welcome!
